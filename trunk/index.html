It was 1998 when I stepped in my first class, nursery school. I looked very bright (and by the way I was…) since I managed to become number one at the end of the year. I was rewarded a present, or a gift, or a kettle, a big kettle to be precise. Since then, (you better be ready for this…) since that time I was number one, the closest I’ve ever been to number one was when I was the leading student in geography (which I chose because I wanted to do something related to aviation – to be a pilot!!!) in Chinga boys High School. I know I possibly will never be the first in anything, given the well proven fate in poetry, athletics, soccer, martial arts, academics, conduct, personality, music including many more….but I also know at least I tried all I could, and was never the last. In fact, am always among the top half!!

Now am in campus [11:58 am; June 14, 2014], where I am happily enjoying my fate. I am taking Software Engineering, which in fact, I consciously decided to choose while in high school. Yeah, I know you question…I talked of wanting to be a pilot. Here is the story that resulted to change of a dream, change of plan is a better substitute. And I was like, “me and planes, we are divorced, divorced for real”.
